For Visitors

So, you've found this place. I'm sorry that this place is not a bonefire for you. It's a personal bonefire for myself. A rest place. A piece of mind in a passed space and time. But I guess I can never call a bonefire to be mine. So, welcome, sit down, take few deep breathe, leave your mind from the busying reality. When you're ready, feel free to explore this place as you like.

For Those who Wander
